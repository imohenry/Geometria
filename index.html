What does it mean to be "fit and healthy"

You are probably at that point in your life, you think you need to get fit and stay healthy, but what does it really mean. Whether you want to build muscle or transform your body, it all boils down to two factors.


Your resolve and body type. Now let's break it down shall we; In getting fit or staying healthy, your mind has to be in tune with what ever decision you decide to take, why? because starting a journey you won't finish is extremely unhealthy and leads to a lot of emotional issues.

Now what do we mean by "body type" and how does it count as a factor, you might be thinking, "then what am i doing here?". Every human ever born has a body type which is as a result of environmental and genetic modifications over time, certain humans find it difficult to lose weight while some are yearning for a little increase in their weight. This disparity in humans is the foundation of Geometria, we have regimens and programs for everyone.
